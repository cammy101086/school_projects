Enjoy the Recipe of the Week!

Buffalo style cauliflower

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Mix 1 head of cauliflower (broken into smaller pieces) with about 1tbsp of olive oil. Then have the following seasoning: 1tsp of garlic powder, 1tsp of paprika, 1tsp of cayenne powder, and 1tsp of salt. Mix the seasoning with the cauliflower. Bake in oven for 20 mins. When finish baking toss with 1.5 tbsp with your favorite hot sauce.
