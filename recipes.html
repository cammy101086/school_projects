Double Stuffed Peanut Butter Cups

Ingredients:

  1. Dark Chocolate Bar
  2. Natural Peanut Butter
  3. Honey-4Tbs

Directions:

Melt a dark chocolate bar in microwave. When it is all melted, pour half into 2 cupcake holders. Then put them in freezer for about 10 min. Mix together 4Tbs all natural peanut butter (or almond butter), and 1tsp honey. Put that mixture on top of chocolate and put the rest of the melted chocolate on top. Place back in freezer for at least 30 mins

Chocolate Chip Protein Cookies

Ingredients:

  1. 1 Large Egg
  2. 1/2 Cup of Chocolate Myofusion Protein Power
  3. 1/4 Cup of Coconut Flour
  4. 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda
  5. 1/4 Cup of Flaked Coconut
  6. 1/4 Cup of Milk
  7. 10 Ground Brazil Nuts
  8. 1/8 Cup Sugar-Free Dark Chocolate Chips

Directions:

Preheat oven to 340 F or 170 C. Mix together egg, protein powder, coconut flour, baking soda, coconut, milk, and ground nuts. Once dough is formed, stir in chocolate chips. Using a spoon, divide mixture into 12 cookies and place on a sprayed cookie sheet. Put in oven for 15-20 minutes. Remove them from the oven as soon as they cook through. It's good if they're a bit soft in the center. Make sure you don't overcook them because they'll dry up and lose their charm if you do. Let them cool, pour yourself a tall glass of milk and boom!
